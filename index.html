About the Achievement System

This system tracks your study progress and rewards you with achievements as you reach milestones. Each achievement falls into one of several categories and contributes to your overall achievement points.

How it works

As you study, the system automatically tracks your progress. When you meet the conditions for an achievement, it will be marked as completed in your profile. Your total achievement points determine your rank.
